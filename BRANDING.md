# Onza Brand Identity

## 1. Brand Manifesto
* At Onza, we believe wealth should bring clarity, not chaos.
* We design strategies that create calm today - and certainty for tomorrow.
* True wealth is not measured by numbers alone, but by the freedom, security, and legacy it creates.
+2

Quiet confidence. Lasting futures. Wealth with design.

## 2. Typography
* Primary Font: Hanken Grotesk .

Usage: Headlines use Hanken Bold; Subheaders and Body Copy use Hanken Light; Highlights use Extralight Italic .
+1

## 3. Brand Colors & Gradients
* Deep Modern (Base): #13181c * Teal Scape (Primary/Secondary): #0a5060, #28a0aa, #3cc2cc * Sand & Warmth (Complimentary/Highlight): #ffdc81 (Warm Gold), #f2e9da (Light Zen), #ccc1b4 * Primary Gradient: Linear transition from Modern (#13181c) to Quiet Luxury (#3cc2cc) to Warmth (#ffdc81) to Light Zen (#f2e9da) .
+4

## 4. UI / Visual Language
* The Onza graphic language relies on gradients and color, prioritizing depth and a sense of space over flat designs.
* The aesthetic is 'Quiet Luxury'. Maintain a minimalist, highly curated, and airy feel across all components.
